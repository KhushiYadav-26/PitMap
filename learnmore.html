A Smart Road Safety App: Detecting Potholes and Road Irregularities

The Problem

Roads with unnoticed potholes lead to frequent accidents, vehicle damage, and traffic congestion. Manual reporting is inefficient, and authorities are not alerted in real-time. There’s no centralized tracking system for road conditions.

Our Solution

PitMap uses AI and crowdsourcing to detect and report road hazards in real time. Integrated with government systems for rapid resolution, it improves road safety for all.

Key Features

AI-Based Detection

Uses smartphone cameras & sensors to detect potholes automatically.

GPS Mapping

Accurately geotags detected potholes with precise location data.

Real-Time Alerts

Notifies drivers and authorities instantly about dangerous road hazards.

How PitMap Works

1️⃣ Drive with app running
2️⃣ AI detects pothole
3️⃣ Location sent to authority
4️⃣ Repair tracked in real-time

Benefits

  • ✅ Safer roads & fewer accidents
  • ✅ Real-time alerts for drivers
  • ✅ Automated tracking for authorities
  • ✅ Data-driven road maintenance
