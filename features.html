Our Powerful Features

PitMap is packed with tools to make road safety smarter and faster.

AI Pothole Detection

Uses smartphone sensors and camera to automatically detect potholes.

GPS Geotagging

Accurately tags pothole locations for mapping and reporting.

Authority Dashboard

Gives municipal teams a live view of reported potholes and repair status.

Real-Time Alerts

Notifies drivers and authorities about dangerous road conditions instantly.

Reward System

Users earn points for reporting potholes, redeemable for fuel discounts.

Smart Navigation

Suggests safer, pothole-free routes using AI and live traffic data.
